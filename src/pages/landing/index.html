Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Thoughtfully designed features that just work — no noise, no bloat.

Real-time Messaging
Socket-powered instant delivery. Messages arrive before you even finish thinking about sending them. Typing indicators, seen receipts, and delivery confirmation — all in real time.
Smart Archive
Tuck conversations away without losing them. One tap to revisit.
Security First
JWT auth, CSRF protection, rate limiting, and bcrypt. Every layer hardened.
Message Reactions
Express more with less. React to any message with a single tap — quick, expressive, human.
Pinned Messages
Keep what matters at the top. One-tap access to pinned messages in a beautiful dedicated view.
Instant Search
Search contacts and messages simultaneously. Tap a result, jump right there.
PWA Ready
Install once, use everywhere. No app store required — just open and add to home screen.
Accent Themes
Your chat, your color. Switch accent themes and wallpapers instantly.
